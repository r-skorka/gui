Test mode keybinds:

  • Global
    • [Backquote] - Toggle keybinds list
  • KKM Screen
    • [Spacebar] - insert/remove KKM
    • [O] - toggle online ticket availability
  • Payment Screen
    • [1],[2],[5] - insert coins
    • [z],[x],[c],[v],[b] - insert bills (10,20,50,100,200)
    • [A]/[D] - Accept/Decline credit card payment
    • [C] - confirm blik payment
